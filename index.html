AMANI FESTIVAL

7th edition 2020

A unique experience alongside traditional dancers and artists from the region (Rwanda, Burundi, DRC, etc.); drummers from Burundi; acrobats; young entrepreneurs & artisans from Goma; associations & NGOs; runners via Sunday Ekiden

From Friday 14 to Sunday 16 February 2020

3 days of song, dance, party, discovery and cultural meetings! At Mwanga College in Goma, RDC

main program
